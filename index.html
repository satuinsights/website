About us

Satu Insights is a collective of experienced market research consultants with over 15 years of expertise, serving clients globally from our base in Southeast Asia.

Our name, "Satu," means "One" in Malay and Indonesian, symbolizing our commitment to being the single, trusted research partner clients turn to for insightful, high-quality research solutions.

At Satu Insights, we focus on delivering consistent, integrity-driven research that goes beyond simply providing data. We uncover real, actionable insights to help our clients make informed decisions with confidence.
